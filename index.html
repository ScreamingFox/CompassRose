Navigate your way to a quite beach house located in a beautiful setting. Walk to the beach within minutes. This beach house is perfect for a couple or family looking for quite getaway. Beach house comes with one queen bed and pull out sofa. Amenities include gas fireplace, level 2 car charger, free wifi, smart TV, beautiful kitchen, washer, dryer, and self check-in. Located minutes from Ocean Park. There is no shortage of things to do on the Long Beach Peninsula.

Large bedroom perched above the living room. There are 3 stairs to access the bedroom. The large living room has a gas fireplace, large bay windows, and a sliding glass door that leads to the deck. Kitchen includes custom countertops and custom cabinets. Full bath with tub and shower.
